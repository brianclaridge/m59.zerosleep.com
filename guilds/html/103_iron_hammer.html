IronHammer (103)
Server 103's second oldest guild.
http://www.pcnet.com/~dochris/iron.htm

The mission of IronHammer is to ensure that a peaceful environment is maintained for the enjoyment of all the honorable and good citizens of Meridian 59. The guild will defend innocents against unfair treatment. It will always help new citizens and provide sufferance to those in need. Citizens are invited to come to IronHammer when in need of assistance. IronHammer is a family environment, where members may find help in their progress and advancement. We want our members to be the most capable people in the world. We are interested in your welfare and continued successful existence in our fine community.
