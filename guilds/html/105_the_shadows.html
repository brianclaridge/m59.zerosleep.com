The Shadows (105)
The Shadows are a group of individuals that have come together to help each other have a more enjoyable experience. We look to help others in need and to acquire information for everyone to benefit from. We are not a political guild, nor do we look to tell anyone how he or she must act. We feel that people are free to act, as they will as long as it does not adversely interfere with other people. Sages, scholars, and carefree adventures are all welcome in our guild.

We are the people in the "shadows" and have never been a vocal group, but we have been around since the beginning. Call on us in times of need and we will try to do our best to help.
