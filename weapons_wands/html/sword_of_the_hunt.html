Sword of the Hunt

This weapon is truly a fine piece of workmanship. Intricate organic carvings intertwine along the hilt, enclosing an exquisitely beautiful gem, almost as if it were an eye gazing calmly back at you. Indeed, every now and then out of the corner of your eye you almost catch it blinking.

Info & Tips
    Info Provided by Sensei (102)
  • Source: Dungeon of Castle Brax
  • Mendable: Mends Itself, Never breaks
  • Enchant: No
  • Tip #1: Must retain postive karma or the SOTH kills you.
  • Tip #2: The sword gives you vigor from any monsters killed on the screen your on.
  • Tip #3: You can safely lose the sword by taking a Token Death
  • Tip #4: Sword can be unequipped but not dropped.
  • Tip #5: Sword hits based on karma. High so far of 17 HP.
  • Tip #6: Sword reappears every 6 - 12 hours in one of the three nooks in the Dungeon of Castle Brax..
  • Magical Find: No
