Princess Kateriina
Age has made Princess Kateriina stronger and more certain of herself, but has done little to temper her naivete. She has developed great disdain for the Duke and all who engage in political maneuvering. She considers matters of political intrigue to be below her, trusting that if she rules wisely and justly all will accept her. Magic continues to fascinate her and she sometimes dreams of running away to study the arcane arts.
