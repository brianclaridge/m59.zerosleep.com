Gaze of the Basilisk
Turns the target of the spell to stone, so long as you have the concentration to keep them that way. Requires yrxl sap and the blood of a shaman to cast. Casting this spell can cause you to be labeled an outlaw.

Info & Tips
  • Level: 6
  • Reagents:
    • 1 Yrxl Sap
    • 1 Shaman Blood

  • Teacher: Maleval
  • Cost: 16000
