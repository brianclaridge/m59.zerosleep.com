Blood Inheritance
Breaks the supernatural bond between an item and its owner. To break the bond, one vial of solagh and two of shaman blood must be poured over the item, then the casters' mystical energy must be focused through a prism.

Info & Tips
  • Level: 5
  • Reagents:
    • 1 Prism
    • 1 Vial of Solagh
    • 2 Shaman Blood
  • Teacher: Lich
  • Cost: 8000
  • Use: Breaks the connection to a Sword of the Hunt or Amulet of the Three.
