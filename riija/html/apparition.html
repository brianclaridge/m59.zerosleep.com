Apparition
Summons an illusory creature to attack your enemies. The creature does good damage, but is easily banished. Requires web moss and rainbow fern to cast. Casting this spell can cause you to be labeled an outlaw.

Info & Tips
  • Level: 4
  • Reagents:
    • ?? Web Moss
    • ?? Rainbow Fern

  • Teacher: Monk of Riija
  • Cost: 4000
