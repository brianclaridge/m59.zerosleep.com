Rose
This is a simple, yet elegant and delicate, rose.

Info & Tips
  • Source: NPC Favors
  • Use #1: The Rose increases Mysticism by +5. Thus allowing for a higher softcap for magicians. One can sometimes be found in the basement crates in Castle Victoria.
  • Use #2: Drop it at the base of the Mimosa tree in Marion. Apparently it increases your Mysticism for two hours. Other than that, my info is scarce.
  • Tip #1: Rumor has it that it helps under 40 HP players drastically with improving in spell %.
