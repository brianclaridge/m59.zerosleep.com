The Insurrection Wand List
Wand of Seduction
  • Use: Seduces a beast of your choice. Once cast, it takes 10 mana every five seconds to hold the seduction.
  • Source: Courier Quests, ????
Wand of Striking
  • Use: It does minimal damage. 'The slitherbolt strikes Conan!'
  • Source: Orc Wizard, Lupogg
Wand of Immobilization
  • Use: Holds the target in place like Qor's Hold spell. You must have -50 karma to use it.
  • Source: Ghost of Far'nohl, Lupogg, Shadowbeast, Frisconar Favors
Wand of Destruction
  • Use: Detroys an item carried by the target. Similar to Faren's Shatter spell.
  • Source: Narthyl Worm, ????
Wand of Purification
  • Use: Removes all player enchantments from target. Similar to Shal'ille Purfiy spell.
  • Source: Yeti, Avar Chieftains
Wand of Lightning
  • Use: Casts a bolt of lightning at the target. Similar to Faren's Lightning Bolt spell.
  • Source: Ghost of Far'nohl, Living Tree, Narthyl Worm
