Truth
The words of this song form an enchantment which disallows the casting of spells in the illusionary art. Two emeralds are required to begin performing this song.

  • Mana Cost: 3 initially then 3 per 5 seconds

  • Teacher: Parrin Arragone
  • Cost: 500
