Nemesis (102)

When you get this message, you lose almost no hit point from the blow you took : trolls hit for 1-2 damage, and so all. It's great to fight avars too.

To get it, equip with plate, ks, helmet (orc one), and gauntlets. Then, you may have the message for a while.

The status of the stuff is important, but everything flawless does not give an automatic bonus. Sometimes, you just get the message when something just loses one step.
