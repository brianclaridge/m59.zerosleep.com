Why the Pricing Change?
THIS WAS TAKEN DIRECTLY FROM 3DO.COM
Q: Why is 3DO changing the price of Meridian 59?

A: Revelation, the newest incarnation of Meridian offers vastly improved graphics, story, monsters, and UI. 3DO is also hard at work on Might and Magic® Online, our next-generation massively multiplayer online RPG. This kind of sustained, high-quality development is expensive. We are changing our pricing structure for Meridian 59 in order to continue to bring you the best possible online games.
