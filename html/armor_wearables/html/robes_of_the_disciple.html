Robes of the Disciple
Heavy and warm, disciple robes typically denote a fervent (and some would say unhealthy) dedication to one school or another.

Info & Tips
  • Source: Orc Wizard/NPC Favors
  • Mendable: Yes
  • Enchant: No
  • Protection vs Magic: ??
  • Magical Find: No

Each robe has a symbol on the back representing the school it is dedicated too. The following is a description of each symbol:
    • Qor: Skull
    • Shal'ille: Ankh
    • Faren: Bolt
    • Kraanan: Weapons
    • Riija: Eye
    • Jala: Harp
