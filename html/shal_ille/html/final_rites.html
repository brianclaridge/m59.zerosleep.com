Final Rites
This is the lengthy formal ceremony performed at funerals to help the recently departed soul pass smoothly on to the Underworld. It may also be used to force nearby undead back in their graves where they belong. Requires two emeralds, which during the ceremony are crushed into a symbolic handful of dust and scattered into the air.

Info & Tips
  • Level: 5
  • Reagents:
    • 2 Emeralds

  • Teacher: Priestess Xianna
  • Cost: 8000
  • Tip #1: Aschere (102), Final Rites can be a lot of fun during a Frenzy if your % is high. Just stand in an inconspicuous part of the map in a busy area (Tos, Cor Noth) and cast until you are discovered. All neg karma players will take damage!
