Monk of Riija/Priestess of Riija

Monk of Riija:
Floating several feet above the ground, this enigma of a being watches you. When it speaks, the monk's voice crackles like century old parchment. You have a very uneasy feeling that all is not as it seems.

Spooky Little Girl:
With a sickly sweet child's voice and an unwholesome look of innocense, the Priestess of Riija welcomes you to her inner sanctum of unreality.
