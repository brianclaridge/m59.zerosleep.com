Hunt
Allows the casters to detect a player's location for a limited time. The hunted player's name must be spoken during the initial trance. When the enchantment fires, for the duration of the spell the hunters will receive periodic notification of the hunted player's whereabaouts. Requires all of the claws from one kriipa paw, and the casters' mystical energies must be focused through a prism.
