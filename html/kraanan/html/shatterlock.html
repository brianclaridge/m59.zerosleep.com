Shatterlock
Penetrates the locks of a guild hall, note that it won't open other kinds of doors elsewhere. Requires Kriipa Claws to cast. Casting this spell can cause you to be labeled an outlaw.

Info & Tips
  • Level: 6
  • Reagents:
    • 4 Kriipa Claw

  • Teacher: Jonas D'Accor
  • Cost: 16000

Melody (102)
