Bramble Wall
Creates a thicket of thorny, twisted bramble. Such a wall would be painful to pass, though it wouldn't much harm one who remained motionless. Requires a kriipa claw to cast. Casting this spell can cause you to be labeled an outlaw.

Info & Tips
  • Level: 2
  • Reagents:
    • ?? Kriipa Claw

  • Teacher: Miriana
  • Cost: 1000
